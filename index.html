Features

Everything you need to
be anywhere.

All the power of pricier tools like iMyFone AnyTo — on iPhone, Android, Mac & Windows, with a real free trial. Pick your transport mode, fly a real flight path, set a schedule, or download maps for offline use.

One-tap Teleport

Drop a pin anywhere on the map — a landmark across the world or the coffee shop down the street — and you're there instantly. Search by name, paste coordinates, or tap the map.

Live Joystick Walk

Move your location in real time with an on-screen joystick — walk, run, bike or drive speed. Push to steer, farther to go faster.

Multi-stop Routes

Chain several stops into one trip and pick how you travel — drive, walk, cycle or public transport. Wander snaps to roads, paces realistic speed, humanizes the motion, and can auto-loop.

Flight Planner

Pick two airports or a time range, then fly the great-circle route between them — off a bundled airport dataset, no API. Boat mode sails open water the same way.

Geofence & Scheduling

Set Wander to be at a place during set hours, and let geofence triggers hand you back to real GPS the moment you actually arrive somewhere. On iPhone & Android.

Offline Maps

Download a map region over Wi-Fi, then pan, zoom and teleport with no connection — OpenStreetMap tiles cached on device. On iPhone & Android.

PoGo Mode

Jump-teleport with a cooldown timer and block-until-cooldown lock — plus presets for Pokémon GO, Monster Hunter Now, Pikmin Bloom & Ingress.
