Features

Everything you need to
be anywhere.

All the power of pricier tools like iMyFone AnyTo — on iPhone, Android, Mac & Windows, with a real free trial.

One-tap Teleport

Drop a pin anywhere on the map — a landmark across the world or the coffee shop down the street — and you're there instantly. Search by name, paste coordinates, or tap the map.

Joystick Walk

Move your location in real time with an on-screen joystick. Push to steer, farther to go faster.

Route Mode

Drive a path with realistic speed, stops, acceleration and a live ETA — like you're really driving it.

PoGo Mode

Jump-teleport for Pokémon GO with a built-in cooldown timer, so you can hop between spots without racing the clock yourself.

How it works

Set up once. Spoof forever.

Here's the iPhone path — a quick one-time setup on your Mac, then it's all on your phone. (Android installs straight from the APK, and the Mac & Windows apps just run.)

Install Wander

Run the one-click Wander Installer on your Mac and sign in with a free Apple ID.

Turn on Dev Mode

Enable Developer Mode on your iPhone and grab the free LocalDevVPN from the App Store.

Connect

Open Wander, connect the tunnel — the setup checklist walks you through anything missing.

Go anywhere

Drop a pin, grab the joystick, or plan a route. Your phone now says you're wherever you want.
