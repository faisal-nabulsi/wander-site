Features

Everything you need to
be anywhere.

All the power of pricier tools like iMyFone AnyTo and Tenorshare iAnyGo — on iPhone, Android, Mac & Windows, with a real free trial.

One-tap Teleport

Drop a pin anywhere on the map — a landmark across the world or the coffee shop down the street — and you're there instantly. Search by name, paste coordinates, or tap the map.

Live Joystick Walk

Move your location in real time with an on-screen joystick, at walk, run, bike or drive speed. Push to steer, farther to go faster.

Multi-stop Routes

Chain several stops into one trip. Wander snaps to roads, paces realistic speed, humanizes the motion, and can auto-loop the whole route.

PoGo Mode

Jump-teleport with a cooldown timer and an optional block-until-cooldown lock. Built-in presets for Pokémon GO, Monster Hunter Now, Pikmin Bloom & Ingress.

Transport Modes

Pick how a route is travelled — driving, walking, cycling or public transport. Transit follows roads at transit speed with realistic dwells at each stop.

Boat & Plane Routes

Cross open water on a great-circle boat path, or fly the great-circle flight route between two points. On Android, plane mode reports real altitude in the air.

Flight Planner

Pick two airports by name, or set a time range and choose from a list of flights, then fly the great-circle route. Runs off a bundled airport dataset — no API, no account.

Reorder & Reverse

Drag to reorder the stops in a multi-stop route, or flip the whole thing end-to-end with a one-tap reverse.

3D Globe

Spin a full 3D globe and tap any spot to teleport there — a fast, playful way to hop across the planet.

Street View Preview

Peek at a spot in Street View before you commit, so you land exactly where you meant to.

Satellite & Hybrid Maps

Switch between standard, satellite and hybrid map views to find the exact rooftop, trail or street corner.

Local Time & Weather

See the live local time and weather at your spoofed spot — plus a heads-up if your device's time zone doesn't match.

Smart Search

Find any place by name or address, paste raw coordinates, or drop a Plus Code — Wander takes you straight there.

AI Natural-Language Teleport

Just say where you want to be — "take me to Times Square" — and Wander finds the spot and drops you there.

Favorites & History

Organize favorites into folders with tags and notes, browse your full teleport history, and undo the last jump with a tap.

Record & Replay

Record a real route as you actually move, then replay it any time — perfect for repeatable, believable paths.

Save-loops & Itinerary

Save a route and run it on repeat, or queue a timed itinerary — "stay 1 hour here, then route to there" — and let it play out.

AI "Believable Day"

Type a prompt or hit surprise-me and Wander generates a natural-looking day of movement for you to run.

Raids, Eggs & Events

A read-only board of current Pokémon GO raids, eggs and events, so you know where the action is before you hop.

Scheduling

Set Wander to be at a place during set hours and it starts and stops on its own. On Android it runs even when the app is closed; on iPhone it keeps going in the background.

Geofence Triggers

Wander snaps back to your real GPS the moment you actually arrive at — or leave — a real place, so your device is honest whenever it needs to be. On iPhone & Android.

GPX Import & Export

Bring your own tracks or export the ones you build — Wander walks or drives the whole path with realistic speed.

Sync, Backup & Restore

Sync your saved places across devices, and back up or restore everything you've saved with one tap.

9-Language Localization

Use Wander in Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese or Russian.

Panic Button

One tap snaps you instantly back to your real GPS — no digging through menus when you need out fast.

Distance-jump Smoothing

Big teleports glide across the gap over a few seconds instead of snapping, so apps can't flag an impossible instant jump — handy for dating apps and Life360. On iPhone & Android.

Weather-aware Pacing

Routes ease off the pace when it's raining or snowing at your spoofed spot, so your movement matches the conditions. On iPhone & Android.

Frozen & Approximate Location

Hold perfectly still with a frozen toggle, or blur yourself to an approximate ~3–5 km area for privacy. On iPhone & Android; desktop holds still via the fluctuation toggle.

Anti-detection Tips

A heads-up when your device's time zone doesn't match your spoofed spot, plus clear guidance on lining up your VPN and IP so the network agrees with your location.

Dark Mode

A full dark theme that's easy on the eyes for late-night sessions, alongside the standard light look.

Wi-Fi or Cellular

No Wi-Fi? Flip on Airplane Mode and Wander keeps working on cellular — spoof from anywhere.

Runs Untethered

After setup, no computer needed. Wander re-signs and refreshes itself over the air, so it never expires on you.

Offline Maps

Download a map region over Wi-Fi, then pan, zoom and teleport across it with no connection — OpenStreetMap tiles, cached on device. On iPhone & Android.

Android Tile & Widget

On Android, teleport in one tap from a Quick Settings tile or a home-screen widget — no need to open the app.

Multi-device beta

From the Mac or Windows app, hold one location across several iPhones at once — great for keeping a group in sync.

VPN / IP Alignment

An optional guide to line up your IP with your spoofed spot, for apps that also check your network location.

Auto-Updates

New versions download and install themselves over the air — you're always on the latest build.
