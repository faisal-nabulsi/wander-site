Purchase FAQ

Everything about pricing, payment, and how the license works — answered straight.

Yes — there's no lock-in. The lifetime license is a one-time payment, so there's nothing to cancel. For monthly or yearly Pro you simply stop paying when you're done — since you pay by Venmo, nothing auto-charges your card. You're never on a recurring billing hook.

No jailbreak required — Wander runs on a stock iPhone, so you're not weakening your device's security. There's a real free trial so you can test it end-to-end before paying a cent, and we're openly honest that Wander is an actively developed work in progress. Try it first, decide for yourself.

Right now payment is via Venmo — @faisal_nabulsi. Once your payment comes through, you get a license key emailed or DM'd back to you. That one key activates Pro on both iOS and desktop.

Start with the free trial — it lets you confirm Wander works for your setup before you pay. If you buy a paid key and it genuinely doesn't work, reach out on Discord and we'll get you a fix or a refund. We'd rather sort it out than leave you stuck.

A license binds to one phone to keep keys from being shared around. Got a new phone? Just contact us for a re-issue and we'll move your key over. The same key also works on the desktop app — iOS and desktop share one license.
