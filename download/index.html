Install guide

How to install — pick your platform

Full step-by-step for every platform. Android installs in seconds with no computer; iPhone is a one-time ~10-minute setup.

📱 iPhone — install the Wander app

One-time ~10-minute setup using a Mac. No jailbreak. After that your iPhone runs on its own — you won't need the computer again.

  1. On your Mac, open the Wander Installer (download above) and sign in with your free Apple ID — no paid developer account needed.
  2. Click Install Wander. It signs the app and installs it onto your connected iPhone automatically.
  3. Trust the app: the first launch shows "Untrusted Developer." Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management, tap your Apple ID email, and tap Trust. Then turn on Settings → Privacy & Security → Developer Mode and restart when asked.
  4. Get the tunnel app: install LocalDevVPN (free) from the App Store — it's the secure tunnel Wander uses. Connect it on Wi-Fi, or turn on Airplane Mode first and then connect to run on cellular.
  5. Open Wander → Get Started, then drop a pin, grab the joystick, or run a route. Wander re-signs itself, so you won't need the computer again.
💡 Only have a Windows PC, no Mac? You can still spoof your iPhone — open the Windows tab: Wander Desktop drives your tethered iPhone straight from your PC, with nothing to install on the phone.

🤖 Android — install the APK

No computer, no root — it installs straight on your phone in under a minute.

  1. On your Android phone, tap Download APK above (or open this page on the phone).
  2. Open the downloaded Wander.apk. Android asks to allow installs from your browser — enable "Install unknown apps" for that browser, then go back and open the APK again.
  3. Tap Install, open Wander, and you're set — teleport, joystick, routes & PoGo Mode, no jailbreak or root.
💡 The "unknown apps" permission is per-app and only used for installing — you can switch it back off afterward. Wander auto-updates itself from inside the app.

💻 Mac — Wander Desktop

Spoof a tethered iPhone straight from your Mac. Nothing gets installed on the phone.

  1. Download Wander for Mac above, unzip it, and drag Wander Desktop into your Applications folder.
  2. First launch only: right-click the app → Open → Open to clear the unsigned-app warning. If macOS says "damaged," run the xattr -cr command in the yellow box above — that always fixes it.
  3. Open Wander Desktop and enter your Mac password once — it needs admin just to open the secure tunnel to your iPhone. No Terminal needed.
  4. Plug your iPhone in (or put it on the same Wi-Fi), click Connect Device, then teleport, joystick, or run a route — all from your Mac.
💡 Works on Apple Silicon & Intel. Password prompt didn't appear? Launch from Terminal: sudo "/Applications/Wander Desktop.app/Contents/MacOS/Wander Desktop"

🪟 Windows — Wander Desktop

The same desktop spoofer for Windows — it drives your tethered iPhone from your PC.

  1. Install the Apple driver first: get Apple Devices from the Microsoft Store (or iTunes from apple.com) so Windows can talk to your iPhone over USB.
  2. Download Wander-Windows.zip above and unzip the folder.
  3. Run Wander Desktop.exe. It's an unsigned indie build, so SmartScreen may warn — click "More info" → "Run anyway." Approve the admin (UAC) prompt — it's needed for the device tunnel.
  4. Plug your iPhone in, click Connect Device, then teleport, joystick, or run a route — all from your PC.
💡 If Connect Device can't see your iPhone, tap "Trust This Computer" on the phone and make sure Apple Devices / iTunes is installed.
