⚠️ macOS says "damaged" or "can't be opened"? It's not damaged — that's just macOS blocking an app that isn't signed with a paid Apple account. Fix it once, two ways:
Option 1 — Terminal (most reliable). Drag the app into Applications, then paste this and press Return:
xattr -cr /Applications/Wander\ Installer.app
For the desktop app, use xattr -cr /Applications/Wander\ Desktop.app instead.
Option 2 — no Terminal. Right-click the app → Open. If it's still blocked, open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down to Security, and click "Open Anyway" next to the Wander message — then confirm with your Mac password or Touch ID. (If it still says "damaged," use Option 1 — that always works.)
Install guide

How to download & set up

Ten minutes, one time, on a Mac. After that your phone runs on its own.

1. Install Wander on your iPhone ⌄
  1. On your Mac, open the Wander Installer (from the Download section above).
  2. Sign in with your free Apple ID — no paid developer account needed.
  3. Click Install Wander. It signs the app and installs it to your connected iPhone automatically.
2. Trust the developer & enable Developer Mode ⌄
  1. The first time you tap Wander, iPhone shows "Untrusted Developer" and won't open it — this is normal for sideloaded apps. To fix it: Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → under Developer App, tap your Apple ID email → Trust "…" → Trust to confirm.
  2. Then turn on Settings → Privacy & Security → Developer Mode → toggle on → restart when asked.
  3. (The Developer Mode toggle only appears after Wander is installed — that's normal.)
  4. Now open Wander — it launches normally from here on.
3. Get the tunnel app ⌄
  1. Install LocalDevVPN free from the App Store — it's the secure tunnel Wander uses.
  2. Connect it on Wi-Fi. No Wi-Fi? Turn on Airplane Mode first, then connect it — works on cellular.
4. Open Wander & go ⌄
  1. Launch Wander and tap Get Started. The setup checklist confirms everything's ready.
  2. Drop a pin, grab the joystick, or plan a route — you're anywhere now.
  3. Wander refreshes itself, so you won't need the computer again.
💻 Prefer your Mac? Set up Wander Desktop ⌄
  1. Download Wander for Mac from the section above, unzip it, and drag Wander Desktop into your Applications folder.
  2. First launch only: right-click the app → Open → Open to clear the unsigned-app warning (it's free & open-source, just not paid-notarized).
  3. Open Wander Desktop and enter your Mac password when it asks — it needs admin just once to open the secure tunnel to your iPhone. No Terminal needed.
  4. Plug your iPhone in (or put it on the same Wi-Fi), click Connect Device, then teleport, joystick, or run a route — all from your Mac.

Password prompt didn't show? You can always launch it from Terminal instead:
sudo "/Applications/Wander Desktop.app/Contents/MacOS/Wander Desktop"
