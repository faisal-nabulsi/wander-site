What's new in Wander

The latest updates across iPhone, Android, Mac & Windows — the changes you'll actually notice.

July 16, 2026

Sign in your way — and bring your own routes

Faster, friendlier sign-in and route imports across the board.

Accounts & sign-in All platforms

  • Sign in with Google is now built right into the app.
  • Forgot password? Reset it from the app in a couple of taps.
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) with any authenticator app — rolling out now for extra account security.
  • Sign in with Apple is ready and coming soon.

Android Android

  • New bottom-tab layout that matches the iPhone app — one familiar Wander everywhere.
  • Street View is now a Pro feature and more secure, with the imagery key handled server-side.
  • Import your GPS routes from GPX, KML, GeoJSON and CSV files.

iPhone iOS

  • Import route files to walk your own paths.
  • A believable Life360 / Find My preset for more natural-looking movement.
  • A cleaner, less-cluttered Route screen.
  • More accurate cooldowns across multiple games — Monster Hunter Now, Pikmin Bloom and Ingress.
  • Fixed a crash that could happen on launch.
July 15, 2026

A fresh installer and a hardened desktop app

Polish for the desktop experience, plus a free 3D globe and lifetime Pro.

Desktop & installer Mac & Windows

  • A Wander-themed installer refresh (installer 2.3.0) — cleaner and clearly ours.
  • Street View hardened on desktop, with the imagery key kept server-side.

Everywhere All platforms

  • A free, self-hosted 3D globe — spin the planet and pick anywhere, at no cost.
  • Lifetime Pro support — buy once, keep Pro for good.
